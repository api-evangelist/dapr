At Roadwork our mission statement is “To create a platform that allows our customers to gain actionable insights from their data without additional operational effort”. We work towards optimizing processes and gaining value out of your data. We can summarize the lifecycle of such a system in the classical data platform approach, where we have three main phases: “Data gathering”, “Data processing” and “Data analytics”.

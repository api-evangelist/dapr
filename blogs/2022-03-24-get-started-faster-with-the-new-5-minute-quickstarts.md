Dapr already has well-rounded Quickstart examples, so why reinvent the wheel? Two frequent asks we heard when using the Quickstarts were “How do I use this Dapr API?" and “Can you show me this in my favorite language?”. Fueled by this feedback, we simplified the getting started experience by focusing on the individual building block APIs (for example, service invocation or pub/sub).

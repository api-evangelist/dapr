A new Dapr slide deck is available that you can use for your Dapr presentations!
The deck contains new icons for all the Dapr APIs and built-in features, such as security, resiliency, and observability. The presentation style matches the recently redesigned dapr.io website. The slide deck consists of over 140 slides that can be used as a starting point for your presentation. Remove or hide what is not necessary for your audience, and add new slides to add relevance to your story.
